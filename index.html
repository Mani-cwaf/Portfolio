Origami

December 3, 2023

Christmas Tree

Designed by Jo Nakashima, this was a relatively straitforward model, but the steps were repetitive and the paper was very large, so it took some time to treat and fold.

Time Taken Folding: 4 Hours

Paper Used: 50x50cm green and brown double tissue paper treated with carboxymethylcellulose, 15x15 yellow origami paper cut into quarters and arranged into a strip.

October 13, 2023

Phoenix

Designed by Satoshi Kamiya, This was the most difficult model I've made so far. It took a huge and thin sheet with many precreases in the creasepattern and a lot of repetitive and difficult folds.

Time Taken Folding: 15 Hours

Paper Used: 60x60cm double side yellow single tissue paper treated with double layers of carboxymethylcellulose.

December 16, 2023

Grim Reaper

Designed by Miyamoto Chuya, This was a finicky and complex model. It was more interesting than the phoenix and somewhat easier, but time consuming and difficult nonetheless.

Time Taken Folding: 10 Hours

Paper Used: 48x48cm one side black double tissue paper treated with double layers of carboxymethylcellulose.

January 12, 2024

Caduceus

Designed by Tim Rickman, This was a tedius and difficult model. It was certainly interesting, but the 56x56 grid and box pleating method took the longest, and the cmc shaping was difficult, but led to my personal favourite model so far.

Time Taken Folding: 18 Hours

Paper Used: 50x50cm silver and gold double tissue paper treated with double layers of carboxymethylcellulose.
